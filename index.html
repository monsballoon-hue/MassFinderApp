About MassFinder

MassFinder is a stewardship project for Catholics in Western New England — find Mass, Confession and Adoration near you, throughout the region.

What We're Working On

Right now, finding what's happening at Catholic churches in our region means checking dozens of individual bulletins, websites and Facebook pages. Each parish is behind its own wall. We're trying to change that.

MassFinder uses AI to read parish bulletins and pull services, events and schedule changes into one central place — so you can see what's happening across the region without hunting for it.

It's a small effort at digital evangelism: making it easier for Catholics to stay connected to their faith community and discover what's going on beyond their own parish.

This is actively being built, and you'll notice rough edges. We appreciate your patience as it comes together.
